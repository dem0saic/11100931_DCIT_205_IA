Welcome to the Department of Computer Science at the University of Ghana.University of Ghana also known as UG Legon has been at the forefront of computing since Alan Turing, Alonzo Church and John von Neumann were among its residents. Our department is home to about 2 faculty members, with strong groups in theory, networks/systems vision/graphics, architecture/compilers, programming languages, security/policy, machine learning, natural language processing, human-computer interaction, robotics, and computational biology
